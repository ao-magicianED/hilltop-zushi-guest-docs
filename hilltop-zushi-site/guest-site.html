Welcome to Hilltop Zushi

Nestled on a verdant hilltop in Zushi, just 90 seconds from JR Higashi-Zushi Station, Hilltop Zushi is a spacious retreat that blends Japanese warmth with modern comfort. Surrounded by lush greenery and offering panoramic views, this hidden gem accommodates up to 12 guests across 4 beautifully appointed bedrooms. Whether you're planning a family gathering, a friends' getaway, or a creative retreat, Hilltop Zushi provides the perfect sanctuary between the mountains and the sea.
